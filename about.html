The Story of The Other Side Ministry

Founded in 2023, The Other Side Ministry emerged from a heartfelt mission to restore hope and transform lives devastated by alcohol addiction. It was established by Pastor Millicent Allan, her husband Rev. Allan, and their close friend Susan Connelly in the United States.

Our Mission

Driven by faith and compassion, the ministry seeks to provide care, guidance, and long-term support for individuals struggling with addiction. It focuses on holistic recovery, addressing the body, mind, and spirit.

Stories of Transformation

Lives have been changed profoundly through this ministry. Susan Connelly, a recovered individual and co-founder, exemplifies the possibility of change, while Millicent Allan leads the organization with a heart for service.
